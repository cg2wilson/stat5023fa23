We have
\begin{equation*} Y_{ij} = \mu_i + \varepsilon_{ij} \end{equation*}
where \(Y_{ij}\) is the \(j-\)th observation of the treatment level \(i\text{,}\) \(\mu_i\) is the treatment level \(i\text{,}\) and \(\varepsilon_{ij}\) denotes the random error associated with unit \(j\) in treatment level \(i\) such that
\begin{equation*} \varepsilon_{ij} \approx_{iid} N(0,\sigma^2) \end{equation*}
This model is also referred to as a "mean model"
