Parallel Lines Model:
\begin{equation*} Y_{ij} = \alpha_i + \beta X_{ij} + \varepsilon_{ij} \end{equation*}
for \(i=1,...,t\text{,}\) \(j=1,...,n\text{,}\) where
  • \(Y_{ij}\) denotes response for the \(j-\)th replicate of treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\alpha_i\text{:}\) the \(y-\)intercept for treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\beta\text{:}\) the slope (same for all treatment levels)
  • \(X_{ij}\text{:}\) covariate for \(j-\)th replicate of treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\varepsilon_{ij}\text{:}\) random error associated with \(Y_{ij}\) such that \(\varepsilon\overline{\sim}{iid}N(0,\sigma^2_\varepsilon)\)
Non-Parallel Lines Model:
\begin{equation*} Y_{ij} = \alpha_i + \beta X_{ij} + \varepsilon_{ij} \end{equation*}
for \(i=1,...,t\text{,}\) \(j=1,...,n\text{,}\) where
  • \(Y_{ij}\) denotes response for the \(j-\)th replicate of treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\alpha_i\text{:}\) the \(y-\)intercept for treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\beta_i\text{:}\) the slope for treatment level \(i\)
  • \(X_{ij}\text{:}\) covariate for \(j-\)th replicate of treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\varepsilon_{ij}\text{:}\) random error associated with \(Y_{ij}\) such that \(\varepsilon\stackrel{iid}{\sim}N(0,\sigma^2_\varepsilon)\)
