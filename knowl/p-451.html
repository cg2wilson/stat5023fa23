A statistical effects model with this design and treatment structure is given by
\begin{equation*} Y_{ijk} = \mu + \tau_i + \pi_j + (\tau\pi)_{ij} + \varepsilon_{ijk} \end{equation*}
for \(i=1,...,t\text{,}\) \(j=1,...,p\text{,}\) and \(k=1,...,m\) with
  • \(Y_{ijk}\text{:}\) observation at time \(j\) on experimental unit \(k\) of treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\mu\text{:}\) fixed overall mean
  • \(\tau_i\text{:}\) fixed effect of treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\pi_j\text{:}\) fixsed effect of time \(j\)
  • \((\tau\pi)_{ij}\text{:}\) interaction of \(i-\)th treatment and \(j-\)th time
  • \(\varepsilon_{ijk}\text{:}\) random error associated with \(Y_{ijk}\text{.}\) Note: it would not be likely that these are iid in this context.
