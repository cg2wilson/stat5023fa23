Under RCBD, we have the standard error estimates of \(\overline{Y}_{i\cdot}\) and \(\overline{Y}_{i\cdot} - \overline{Y}_{i'\cdot}\text{:}\)
\begin{align*} s_{\overline{Y}_{i\cdot}} \amp = \sqrt{\dfrac{\hat{\sigma}_{block}^2 + \hat{\sigma}_\varepsilon^2}{n}} \\ s_{\overline{Y}_{i\cdot}-\overline{Y}_{i'\cdot}}\amp = \sqrt{\dfrac{2\hat{\sigma}_\varepsilon^2}{n}} \end{align*}
Under these estimated standard errors, we can perform inference as before. For a contrast \(L = \ds \sum_{i=1}^t a_i\tau_i\) such that \(\ds \sum_{i=1}^t a_i = 0\text{,}\) we have
\begin{align*} \hat{L} \amp = \ds \sum_{i=1}^t a_i \overline{Y}_{i\cdot} \\ s_{\hat{L}} \amp = \sqrt{\ds\dfrac{\hat{\sigma}^2_\varepsilon}{n}\sum_{i=1}^ta_i^2} \end{align*}
We wish to test the hypothesis \(H_0: L = 0\) versus \(H_a: L\neq 0\) with
\begin{equation*} t = \dfrac{\hat{L}}{s_{\hat{L}}}\sim t_{(n-1)(t-1)} \end{equation*}
under \(H_0\text{.}\) Thus, reject \(H_0\) if \(|t| \geq t_{\alpha/2,(n-1)(t-1)}\) under significance level \(\alpha\text{.}\)
