We may also note that we have a similar statement to the TSS decomposition in ANOVA:
\begin{equation*} Y_{ij} = \overline{Y}_{\cdot\cdot} + (\overline{Y}_{i\cdot}-\overline{Y}_{\cdot\cdot}) + (Y_{ij} - \overline{Y}_{i\cdot}) \end{equation*}
where
  • \(\displaystyle \overline{Y}_{\cdot\cdot} = \hat{\mu}\)
  • \(\displaystyle \overline{Y}_{i\cdot}-\overline{Y}_{\cdot\cdot} = \hat{\tau_i}\)
  • \(\displaystyle Y_{ij} - \overline{Y}_{i\cdot} = \hat{\varepsilon_{ij}}\)
