Choosing an appropriate structure is challenging and beyond the scope of the course; SAS provides some measures of model fit such as AIC, BIC. The Null Model Likelihood Ratio Test compares VC to the other specified one by testing the hypothesis
\begin{equation*} H_0: \text{VC is appropriate } \end{equation*}
versus
\begin{equation*} H_a: \text{the other is appropriate} \end{equation*}
The outputs can guide us to choose the covariance structure.
