For a random effects model, we consider a one-way treatment structure in a CRD with \(t\) treatment levels corresponding to the random sample of population of possible levels. This gives the model
\begin{equation*} Y_{ij} = \mu _+ \tau_i + \varepsilon_{ij} \end{equation*}
where \(Y_{ij}\) is the \(j\)th observation in treatment level \(i\text{,}\) \(\mu\) is the overall mean (a fixed constant), \(\tau_i\) is the \(i\)th treatment level effect (random such that \(\tau_i\tilde^{iid} N(0,\sigma_\tau^2\))), and \(\varepsilon_{ij}\) is random error associated with \(Y_{ij}\) such that \(\varepsilon_{ij}\tilde^{iid} N(0,\sigma^2_\varepsilon)\text{.}\) We wish to make inferences about all possible levels in the population.
