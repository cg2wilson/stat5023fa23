An alternative way of writing
\begin{equation*} Y_{ij} = \mu_i + \varepsilon_{ij} \end{equation*}
is referred to as an "effects model" such that
\begin{equation*} Y_{ij} = \mu + \tau_i + \varepsilon_{ij} \end{equation*}
where \(\mu\) is the overall mean and \(\tau_i\) is the \(i-\)th treatment level effect.
