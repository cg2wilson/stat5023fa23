To test \(H_0\text{,}\) we develop a test statistic. Consider
\begin{equation*} SS_{H_0}: \text{ Sum of squares under }H_0 \end{equation*}
Set
\begin{align*} L_1 \amp := \mu_2 - \mu_3 = 0 \\ L_2 \amp := \mu_2 + \mu_3 - 2\mu_4 = 0 \\ L_3 \amp := \mu_2 + \mu_3 + \mu_4 - 3\mu_5 = 0 \end{align*}
Then, we have
\begin{equation*} SS_{H_0} = SSC_{L_1} + SSC_{L_2} + SSC_{L_3} \end{equation*}
due to the mutual orthogonality of the contrasts in \(H_0\text{.}\)
