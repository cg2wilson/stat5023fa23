Suppose we study whether or not there exists any difference on average response in patients having the same therapy among hospitals. A CRD with one-way treatment structure would have us select \(s\) patients at each of \(t\) randomly chosen hospitals. The hospital has randomly selected treatment levels, which are normally distributed as \(N(0,\sigma^2_{hospital})\text{;}\) patients are randomly chosen experimental units unique to each hospital, normally distributed as \(N(0,\sigma^2_\varepsilon)\text{.}\) Here, each experimental unit can be nested within a treatment level.
