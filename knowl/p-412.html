A statistical effects model with a treatment structure of one-way with \(t\) levels, as a CRD with \(n\) replications and \(s\) subsamples can be given as
\begin{equation*} Y_{ijk} = \mu + \tau_i + \varepsilon_{(i)j} + \delta_{(ij)k} \end{equation*}
for \(i = 1,...,t\text{,}\) \(j = 1,...,n\text{,}\) and \(k = 1,...,s\text{,}\) where
  • \(Y_{ijk}\) is the observation on subsample \(k\) of experimental unit \(j\) in treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\mu\) is the fixed overall mean
  • \(\tau_i\) is a fixed effect of treatment level \(i\)
  • \(\varepsilon_{(i)j}\) is random error due to the \(j-\)th experimental unit within treatment level \(i\text{.}\) \(\varepsilon_{(i)j}\sim^{iid} N(0,\sigma_\varepsilon^2)\)
  • \(\delta_{(ij)k}\) is the random error due to the \(k-\)th subsample in experimental unit \(j\) within treatment level \(i\text{.}\) \(\delta_{(ij)k}\sim^{iid} N(0,\sigma_\delta^2\)
