The design above is a RCBD with \(n\) blocks. The treatment structure is one-way with \(t\) levels. We have the following statistical model associated with the table:
\begin{equation*} Y_{ij} = \mu + \tau_i + \beta_j + \varepsilon_{ij} \end{equation*}
where \(i=1,...,t\) and \(j=1,...,n\text{,}\) \(Y_{ij}\) is the observation in the \(j-\)th block receiving the \(i-\)th treatment level, \(\mu\) is the overall mean (a fixed constant), \(\tau_i\) is the \(i-\)th treatment level effect (a fixed constant), \(\beta_j\) is the \(j-\)th treatment block effect such that \(\beta_j \sim^{iid} N(0,\sigma^2_{block})\text{,}\) and \(\varepsilon_{ij}\) is a random error associated with \(Y_{ij}\) such that \(\varepsilon_{ij}\sim^{iid} N(0,\sigma^2_\varepsilon)\)
