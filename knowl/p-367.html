As a quick example, consider this situation: A consumer organization wants to evaluate three major brands, \(B_1,B_2,B_3\text{,}\) of room air cleaners. In order to make the ratings as realistic as possible, they choose to evaluate the air cleaners outside the testing laboratory in residential homes. To control for variations due to the differening air qualities in the homes and due to the time-of-year characteristics of external air pollution, they use a cleaner of each brand in each of three homes and to run the tests at three different months.
